BADMINTON

Singles and doubles courts are available. Book your courts as Soon AS Possible!

Rules and regulations of courts and other equipments:

 Compulsory sports shoes are allowed on court. Player must carry their own rackets. Shuttle Cocks and net will be provided by turf. Weekend available courts are only for doubles or mixed pairs. No single matches will be conducted.

FOOTBALL

A match is played by two teams, each with a maximum of seven to eight players; one must be the goalkeeper. If any team is incomplete with oponent team then please mention in the booking form, turf will inform you if there is any other such team is available. Football and Goal net will be provided by turf.



VOLLEYBALL

A match is played by two teams, each with a maximum of 6 players. If any team is incomplete with oponent team then please mention in the booking form, turf will inform you if there is any other such team is available.

Rules and regulations of courts and other equipments:

 Maximum of three (3) hits per side. Player may not hit the ball twice in succession. Ball may be played off the net during a volley and on serve. Volleyball will be provided by turf.

Cricket

A typical Cricket game has 11 players in each team, maximum team should have 8 players. If any team is incomplete with oponent team then please mention in the booking form, turf will inform you if there is any other such team is available.

Rules and regulations of courts and other equipments:

 Underarm bowling is compulsory, depending on whether female players are in the team. The ball should be pitched beyond the no-ball line, otherwise, it will be called no-ball. Bowler has to ball within the given box. Six is not allowed in turf cricket. .Tennis and seson balls, bats, stumps, pads will be provided by turf.
